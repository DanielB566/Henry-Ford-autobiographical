Henry Ford

Henry Ford was an American industrialist and founder of the Ford Motor Company, revolutionizing the automotive industry by introducing assembly line production techniques and making automobiles affordable for the masses. His innovations profoundly influenced modern manufacturing and transportation. Ford's visionary approach not only transformed the way cars were manufactured but also played a pivotal role in shaping the landscape of American society, contributing to the widespread accessibility of personal transportation and fostering a new era of mobility and economic growth.
